Purpose

Enhance interaction with current and potential customers by providing an online experience that is informative, accessible, and engaging.

Audience

Our primary target audience for the rafting website consists of adventure-seeking individuals aged 18 to 45, who are passionate about outdoor activities and seeking thrilling experiences. These individuals are likely to have a moderate to above-average income and are open to spending on recreational activities.
